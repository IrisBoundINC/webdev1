List of all films

  • Godzilla 2000 (1999) - Godzilla arrives once again in an all new Millennium reboot! But... he's not alone...?
  • Godzilla VS Megaguirus (2000) - Godzilla fights a strange scorpion-mosquito looking kaiju named Megaguirus
  • Godzilla, Mothra and King Ghidorah: Giant Monsters All-Out Attack (2001) - Three of the most iconic kaiju of the franchise fight in an epic brawl of the century
  • Godzilla Against MechaGodzilla (2002) - Tokyo's military build a mechanized Godzilla around the skeletons of the original 1954 Godzilla and sends it to defeat the current Godzilla
  • Godzilla Tokyo SOS (2003) - MechaGodzilla is sent again to stop Godzilla but with the aid of Mothra and her offspring!
  • Godzilla: Final Wars (2004) - This is it! Godzilla fights off every single kaiju that has every existed in his franchise one by one... and comes face to face with an even greater foe...
