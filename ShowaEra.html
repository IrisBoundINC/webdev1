List of all films

  • Godzilla (1954) - The original that started it all, Godzilla attacks Tokyo, Japan after created by the Hiroshima and Nagasaki atom bomb explosion sights
  • Godzilla Raids Again (1955) - Godzilla strikes back in Japan, along with a new monster named Aguirus
  • King Kong Vs Godzilla (1962) - Godzilla dukes it out with the eight wonder of the world, Kong!
  • Mothra Vs Godzilla (1964) - A giant moth creature named Mothra appears to fight Godzilla and tries to stop him from causing mass destruction!
  • Ghidorah the Three Headed Monster (1964) - A three-headed dragon from space arrives and wreaks havoc on earth and it's up to Godzilla, Rodan, a pterodactyl kaiju, and Mothra to stop it
  • Invasion of Astro-Monster (1965) - Aliens from Planet X are attacked by Monster Zero, so Earth sends Godzilla and Rodan to stop it and save Planet X
  • Ebirah, Horror of the Deep (1966) - Godzilla, Mothra, and a crustacean monster named Ebirah, battle on a vacant island
  • Son of Godzilla (1967) - The giant lizard kaiju reveals to have a son named Minilla, and helps Minilla train to become just like his old man
  • Destroy All Monsters (1968) - Eleven kaiju duke it out on Monster Island and find themselves with King Ghidorah. They all team up to fight Ghidorah
  • All Monsters Attack (1969) - Minilla faces a bully kaiju, Gabbara and must confront him in order to get stronger
  • Godzilla VS Hedorah (1971) - Godzilla fights a smog monster, Hedorah, who is created from all types of pollution to the earth!
  • Godzilla Vs Gigan (1972) - Godzilla and Anguirus team up to fend of a new monster from space, Gigan, who has teamed up with King Ghidorah
  • Godzilla VS Megalon (1973) - Godzilla teams up with a size-changing robot named Jet Jaguar to fight off a beatle kaiju, Megalon, who teams with Gigan
  • Godzilla VS MechaGodzilla (1974) - Godzilla is put up with a robot version of himself made by the aliens from Planet X to kill Godzilla
  • Terror of MechaGodzilla (1975) - Aliens retreive MechaGodzilla's body and rebuilds him in hopes to defeating the big G himself
